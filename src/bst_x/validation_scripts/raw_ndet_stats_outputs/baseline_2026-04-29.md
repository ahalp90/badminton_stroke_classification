## Notes

- **`ndet=0` at 0%:** RTMDet-nano found at least one person on every
  single frame across all 1.72M frames. That's a clean result — no
  fully-empty frames anywhere.
- **`ndet=1` floor at 0.53% (9,186 frames):** the irreducible per-frame
  failure floor for any heuristic that requires two detections. These
  frames will be zeroed by `apply_heuristic.py` regardless of which
  variant is used.
- **`ndet=16` tail at 1.12% (19,236 frames):** the `N_max=16` cap is
  exercised on a small but non-zero fraction of frames. The court
  proximity / Voronoi pre-filter in the downstream heuristic means
  the two real players are virtually never the detections being
  dropped at the cap; the discards are referees, line judges, ball
  staff, or front-row spectators. `N_max=8` (the previous default)
  would have clipped the bulk of frames, validating the move to 16.
- **Modal `ndet=10` (35.69%), bulk `ndet=9-11` (78.62%):** reflects
  the full broadcast scene mmpose detects — 2 players + chair umpire
  + ~6 line judges + occasional ball staff and visible front-row
  spectators. The raw stage is intentionally wide; player-slot
  selection happens downstream in the heuristic.
